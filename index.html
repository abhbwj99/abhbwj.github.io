III_YEAR_COMPENDIUM

Welcome ! Here is a presentation of all my work over the three years of my design journey. My awareness &
skillsets span through Print Media Design, Branding & Visual Identity Design, Society-Centered Design
& Graphic/Motion Graphic Design. The webspace you are in currently was programmed by me. It was built to foster
all my work & eventually, enabling everyone else to view it. The rest of what I desire to express
takes its course through all my projects.

Apart from all of this, I enjoy coding, playing guitar & going on long motorcycle rides.
